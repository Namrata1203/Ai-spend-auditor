# Go-To-Market & Growth Strategy (GTM.md)

This document maps out our launch, user acquisition, and monetization pipelines for the **AI Spend Auditor**, designed to feed high-intent leads straight to **Credex**.

---

## 1. Product Positioning & Value Prop

- **The Hook**: "You are overspending on AI. Here's exactly where, and how to get those margins back in 60 seconds."
- **Primary Positioning**: A free, zero-friction financial scanner for startup founders, engineering managers (VPs of Eng/CTOs), and fractional CFOs who are currently experiencing "AI fatigue" on SaaS subscriptions.
- **Lead Generation Conversion Pipeline**:
  1. User fills out current AI stack (anonymous initial entry).
  2. Immediate, high-impact savings visualizer.
  3. AI CFO Summary suggests exactly how much is wasted.
  4. **The Gate**: Captures email to retrieve a permanent shared link, download detailed PDF transcripts, and lock in optimization triggers.
  5. **The Hand-off**: Leads with high savings (>$500/mo) qualify for the bulk-credits consultation program with Credex sales.

## 2. Product Hunt Launch Playbook

- **Product Title**: AI Spend Auditor (by Credex)

## 3. Growth Loops & Acquisition Channels

### Social Share Hook (Viral Loop)
- **Mechanism**: The Dynamic Open Graph metatag replacement.
- **The Loop**: When a founder gets an audit result saving them $2,420/year, they click "Copy Share Link" to post on Twitter/X or Slack with their advisors.
- **Appearance**: Social cards dynamically render:  
  *“Acme Corp can save $2,420/yr on AI tools! Run your spend audit instantly with Credex.”*
  This triggers peer CTOS to run the auditor on their own startups.

### Cold CFO Outbound & VC Networks
- **VC Platform Partnerships**: Provide fractional CFO networks and VC platform leads with the audit tool as a value-add calculator to offer their portfolio startups to extend their runways.
- **Cold Outreach**: Target founders on LinkedIn who just raised seed rounds. Offer a quick audit recommendation check.
