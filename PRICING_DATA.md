# AI Tool Pricing Reference Dataset

This file lists the official pricing numbers utilized inside our **AI Spend Auditor** engine. Every item cited links directly to the vendor's official pricing landing pages, verified as of **May 21, 2026**.

---

## 1. Cursor
- **Pro**: $20/user/month  
  - *Source*: https://www.cursor.com/pricing  
  - *Details*: Includes unlimited slow requests, 500 fast requests.
- **Business**: $40/user/month  
  - *Source*: https://www.cursor.com/pricing  
  - *Details*: Admin controls, SAML/SSO options. Downgrade recommended to Pro for teams under 5 people lacking strict IT control needs.

## 2. GitHub Copilot

- **Business**: $19/user/month  
  - *Source*: https://github.com/features/copilot
- **Enterprise**: $39/user/month  
  - *Source*: https://github.com/features/copilot

## 3. Claude (Anthropic)
- **Pro**: $20/user/month  
  - *Source*: https://claude.ai/pricing
- **Team**: $25/user/month (minimum <b>5 seats</b> required)  
  - *Source*: https://claude.ai/pricing  
  - *Details*: Enforces a flat minimum billing of $125/mo. If a startup is on Team with <5 users, they should downgrade to individual Pro licenses to eliminate ghost-seat waste.
- **API Direct**: Pay-per-token  
  - *Source*: https://www.anthropic.com/pricing
  - *Details*: Sourced bulk arbitrage through Credex yields 25% discount on bulk credit codes.
