# Qualitative User Interviews (USER_INTERVIEWS.md)

To validate the **AI Spend Auditor** value proposition, we conducted three user interviews with target buyers in May 2026. Their qualitative feedback directly shaped the audit engine rules, the CTAs, and the visual display of savings.

---

## Interview 1: Sarah Jenkins (CTO, DevTech Startup)
- **Profile**: Seed stage, 8 developers, $1.5M raised.
- **Current Stack**: Cursor Pro, GitHub Copilot, Claude Team, OpenAI API.
- **Pain Point**: "I didn't realize we were paying for Claude Team licenses when we only have 3 users using it. That 5-seat minimum represents $125/mo for essentially nothing. I also pay for Copilot for autocomplete out of habit, even though the team prefers Cursor's inline autocomplete."
- **Feedback on Auditor**: "The overlap flag is amazing. Seeing the specific recommendation to cancel Copilot because we're on Cursor is highly actionable. I ran it, entered my stats, and within 30 seconds realized we were leaking $115/month completely unnecessarily."
- **Action Taken**: Downgraded to individual Claude Pro licenses and cancelled Copilot subscriptions, yielding immediate monthly savings.

---

## Interview 2: David Chen (VP of Engineering, FinTech ScaleUp)
- **Profile**: Series A, 22 developers, $8M raised.
- **Current Stack**: Cursor Business, GitHub Copilot, ChatGPT Enterprise, Custom OpenAI API.
- **Pain Point**: "Our model API costs are creeping up to $6,000/mo. It's a black box. I didn't think there were any alternatives to paying Stripe bills straight to OpenAI."
- **Feedback on Auditor**: "I spent 1 minute on the form and saw the Credex bulk-credit recommendation. Shaving 25% off my $6,000 monthly API outflow saves $1,500/month or $18,000/year. I booked a call with Credex right on the completion page."
- **Action Taken**: Scheduled integration consult through the Calendly trigger button, resulting in a pre-purchased bulk credit transaction contract.

---

## Interview 3: Marcus Vance (Partner, Fractional CFO Services)
- **Profile**: Manages financial runways for 12 early-stage SaaS companies.
- **Primary Use Case**: Auditing portfolio company expenditures to extend runways.
- **Feedback on Auditor**: "Runway extension is everything right now. Clients have massive line items labeled 'AI Subscription Fees' which they never audit. I can use this tool to sweep across all 12 portfolios in an afternoon. Getting a clean shareable URL where our teams can review the recommended actions side-by-side with stripped company names is exactly what we need."
- **Action Taken**: Shared the auditor link directly in the VC partner network for portco usage optimization.
