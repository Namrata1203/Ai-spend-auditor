## 1. The Credex Credit Arbitrage Model

### The Problem in the Market
Funding rounds are followed by high software overforecasting. Startups lock into massive, multi-year minimum spend API contracts with leading LLM vendors (OpenAI, Anthropic, Google Cloud) to secure custom rates, only to pivot, downsize, or underutilize their allocations. 

These companies are left with millions of dollars in pre-funded, non-refundable token credits that decay over time.

### The Credex Solution (The Arbitrage)
1. **Buy Low**: Credex acquires these distressed, pre-paid corporate API credits from pivoted or over-provisioned companies at **40% to 50% discounts** on the dollar.
2. **Sell High**: Credex bundles these credits and resell them to scaling startups at a **flat 25% retail discount** via secure credit pools with zero API key adjustments required.
3. **Capture the Margin**: Credex secures a **15% to 25% clean spread** of pure margin on every token processed.

```text
  [ Distressed Seller ]  --- (40-50% discount) --->  [  Credex  ]  --- (25% discount) --->  [  Buyer Startup  ]
                                                           |
                                                           +---> [ 15-25% Arbitrage Spread ]
```

## 2. Funnel Economics & Lead Acquisition Costs

Operating a manual sales team to identify startups with heavy API usage is expensive and slow. The **AI Spend Auditor** digitizes top-of-funnel acquisition:

### Target Conversion Funnel
- **Visits**: 10,000 unique hits/mo
- **Completed Audits (Anonymous)**: 4,000 (40% conversion)
- **Lead Capture (Reports Saved via Email Gate)**: 1,200 (30% conversion from audits)
- **High Savings Qualified Leads (Spend > $500/mo API)**: 180 (15% conversion from captured emails)
- **Closed Bulk Credits Buyer Contracts**: 18 (10% sales conversion on qualified leads)

### Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC) Calculation
- **Server and API hosting**: $200/mo (Vite/Node + Gemini token usage)
- **Go-To-Market & Ad Spend**: $2,500/mo (Sponsorships & targeted newsletter ads)
- **Total Acquisition Budget**: $2,700/mo
- **Acquired Customers**: 18
- **Blended CAC**: **$150 per buyer customer**

### Customer Lifetime Value (LTV) Calculation
- **Average scaling billing**: $8,000/mo of api token usage
- **Blended Arbitrage spread margin**: 20% ($1,600/mo gross profit per active startup)
- **Average contract retention**: 12 months
- **LTV**: $1,600 * 12 = **$19,200 gross margins per startup**

### LTV to CAC Ratio
- **LTV / CAC**: **128x** ($19,200 / $150)
This massive unit performance demonstrates why building a highly polished, interactive SaaS Spend Auditor is a high-yield lead generation asset for Credex.
